About Me

Crafting stunning posters, cinematic videos, and immersive 3D worlds for brands and stories.

Namaste! I'm a multidisciplinary creative artist with a passion for visual storytelling. With roots in India's vibrant culture and a vision for the future, I blend traditional aesthetics with cutting-edge technology to create unforgettable experiences.

My work has been featured in international design exhibitions and has helped brands connect with their audience in meaningful ways. I believe in "jugaad" - the art of finding creative solutions to complex problems.
